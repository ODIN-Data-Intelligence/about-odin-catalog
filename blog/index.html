The ODIN Design Manifesto

The data governance tools we use today were designed for a world where having metadata was enough. ODIN is designed for a world where understanding metadata is the only thing that matters. This document explains why — covering meaning vs. naming, standards as architecture, lineage as a live graph, AI contracts, enforced ownership, and the eight principles every design decision is tested against.
